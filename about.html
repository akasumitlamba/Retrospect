About Retrospect

Discover the story behind our curated collection

Our Story

Retrospect was born from a passion for timeless style and quality craftsmanship. We believe that fashion should tell a story - one that connects the past with the present, creating pieces that are both nostalgic and contemporary.

Founded with the vision of bringing back the elegance and attention to detail that defined fashion in previous decades, Retrospect curates collections that celebrate individuality and self-expression. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, comfort, and style.

We're more than just a clothing store - we're a community of fashion enthusiasts who appreciate the artistry behind well-made garments. Our mission is to help you build a wardrobe that reflects your unique personality while standing the test of time.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on quality. Every item in our collection is carefully selected for its superior materials and craftsmanship.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable fashion practices, working with brands that prioritize environmental responsibility.

Community

We believe in building a community of fashion lovers who share our passion for timeless style and quality.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from customer service to the products we offer.

Meet Our Founder

Sumit Kumar Lamba

Hi, I'm Sumit Kumar Lamba, the founder of Retrospect. As a Computer Science graduate from Chandigarh University with a passion for technology and innovation, I bring a unique perspective to the fashion industry by combining technical expertise with creative vision.

With experience as an Associate Quality Engineer at Royal Caribbean Group and expertise in web development, I've applied my technical skills to create Retrospect - a seamless e-commerce platform that prioritizes user experience and quality. My background in software testing and development has helped me build a platform that's not just visually appealing, but also robust and user-friendly.

Retrospect represents my belief that technology should enhance the shopping experience, making quality fashion accessible to everyone. Through my work on projects like Indi Fashion and various web applications, I've developed a deep understanding of what makes an exceptional online shopping experience.
